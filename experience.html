My Intership Experiences

During the previous academic semester, I led an industrial training session at the Tun Razak Library in Ipoh for a total of six weeks. That place has been quite beneficial for me. There are some of the Car Free Day, My Love PTR, and Hi-Tea are three of the most great events that took place during my time working at PTR, and I'd want to use this opportunity to tell everyone about it.

Car Free Day was one of the most important events that was organized and hosted by the Ipoh city council, and we were happy to take part in it. It is a way to encourage individuals to stop using their automobiles for a day and a great chance to minimize the amount of pollution in the air. On our end, we are required to put up an exhibition centered on independence and to conduct a number of contests, including coloring contests, crossword puzzles, word searches, and other such activities. In addition to this, we provide the participants with some interesting gifts to thank them for their participation.

Next, the My Love PTR program in conjunction with Malaysia Day. The event was started with a prayer recitation represented by our staff. Then, there was a speech by our boss and mayor. After that, the event started with variety of costume competition represented by the kindergarten students followed by choral speaking competition by several primary schools. In addition, the choir performance was performed by all PTR staff while waiting for the winner announcement. Before the event ends, motivational and inspirational talk for the sake of the country by Dato Sirajuddin Bin Haji Salleh who is ex-mayor Ipoh.
